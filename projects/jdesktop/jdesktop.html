jDesktop


Apps:

To add apps to your dock click on the green plus and type in a partial name of the app you want to find. Then click on the app to add it to your dock. To remove an app right-click on it in the dock and click "Remove".

Keyboard Shortcuts:

  • ALT+F1 : Show dock
  • ALT+F2 : Run
  • OSKey+R : Run
  • CTRL+ALT+T : Terminal
